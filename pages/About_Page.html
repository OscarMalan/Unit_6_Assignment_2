About Hot Beans

Hot Beans is a website development company that gives YOU the power to control how your website looks. We focus on providing the tools and resources that allow you to set up a website that fits your budget and goals, even if you don't have any coding experience.

Where did the name come from?

The name "Hot Beans" comes from an old anecdote where a pair of brothers were discussing their favorite type of bean. They argued over whose bean was best - string or lima. The more mature brother named a bushel of string beans "Hot Beans". The younger brother name a bushel of lima beans "Hot Beans". The older brother said, "If you're so smart, why don't you go do it yourself?"
